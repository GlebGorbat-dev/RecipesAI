Easy Lasagna

The Easiest Way to Make Lasagna
Many people’s hearts are melted by the great taste of lasagna. It can be made in so many different
ways and with so many variations. In this video and article, we will show you how to make an easy
lasagna that will not require too much work on your part.

Ingredients:
• Chopped onion
• Chopped garlic
• Tomato sauce
• Olive oil
• Ground Meat optional
• Diced tomatoes
• Ricotta cheese or other shredded cheese
• Precooked lasagna noodles
• Seasonings

Instructions For How To Make a Simple Lasagna Delicious! That’s an Easy Lasagna Too!
In this recipe, we make an easy lasagna in a casserole by layering precooked lasagna noodles, cheese,
and a hearty, meaty tomato sauce. Here is a summary:

1. To begin, prepare the tomato sauce by combining sauteed onion, then garlic, in a saute pan over
low to medium heat. Then add in the tomato paste and water, wine, and seasonings. Simmer for the
remainder of the 15 minutes. Once you see your tomato sauce is without any excess water, then you
are done.

2. Ready the cheese(s). We are using ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and Parmesan; this three-cheese
mixture gives the lasagna great flavor, just like the combination of tomatoes.

3. It is just an assembly job after that. A layer of lasagna noodles, a cup of precooked meat, if
using, more lasagna noodles, sauce, and cheese. Repeat this process until you have about three layers
and all ingredients are gone. Pop in a preheated oven to warm, melt, and cook the easy lasagna noodles.

4. Once bubbling, your easy lasagna is ready to eat. Let the easy lasagna cool off a few minutes
before serving.
