Delicious and tender tomato bruschetta is considered the hallmark of Italy.
These crispy sandwiches are famous worldwide. When prepared correctly, this appetizer
should have a fresh, rich flavor. Its components should complement each other.
That's why special attention is paid to the combination of ingredients. Most often, tomato bruschetta
is prepared with the addition of basil or mozzarella. These combinations are considered classic,
meaning they stand the test of time.

Ingredients:
Ciabatta - 4 slices;
Tomatoes - 2 pcs;
Fresh basil - 10 leaves;
Garlic - 2 cloves;
Olive oil - to taste;
Salt and ground black pepper - to taste.

Directions:
Wash and dry the tomatoes, remove the stems. Make a cross-shaped cut on the back.
Scald the tomatoes with boiling water. Then remove the skin. Cut the tomato into quarters,
scoop out the liquid part with the seeds. This can be used for stewing. Dice the pulp and
place it in a deep bowl.
Wash the basil leaves, dry them, and cut them into small pieces. Add them to the tomato.
Add olive oil, salt, and ground black pepper to the filling.
Grind the ciabatta slices with a little olive oil and toast them on the grill or in a frying pan.
As soon as the slices have a delicious crust, rub them with garlic on one side.
Pour the filling onto the bread. Serve the bruschetta immediately!
